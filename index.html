I Never Believed in Love

Whenever I looked around me, I never truly believed in something called “real love.” 💭

To me, most relationships felt like dependency — two people needing each other… until one day the feelings slowly fade away.

I saw breakups. I saw second chances. I saw the same cycle repeating again and again. 🔁

So I made a decision long ago… I would never let myself get deeply involved.

I chose to focus on myself. Stay guarded. Stay practical. 🛡️

Even when I first met you, I never asked for your number. I never asked for your social media.

Not because I didn’t care…

But because I was afraid — that the ending would look just like everyone else’s.

So I built boundaries around my heart. Strong ones. Silent ones.

And I was completely sure… nothing could ever cross them 🤍

And Then… You Happened.

And then… somewhere between conversations and silence… something quietly changed inside me 🌙

I don’t remember exactly when we exchanged numbers. I don’t remember when talking to you became a habit.

I just remember telling myself, “It’s nothing. Just a phase. It will pass.”

So I gave myself time. I tried to stay distant. I tried to convince my heart this was temporary.

But the more I stepped back… the more my thoughts stepped toward you.

You were in my mornings. In my work breaks. In random moments of the day.

And that scared me… because this wasn’t dependency. This wasn’t excitement.

This felt… different 🤍

Now I Understand ❤️

I kept asking myself… Is this just attachment? Is this just emotion? Or is it something deeper?

So I waited. I let the excitement settle. I gave myself time to understand it.

But nothing faded.

My feelings didn’t reduce — they became calmer… but stronger. 🌿

I realized something important.

I don’t want anything from you. I don’t need you to complete me.

I love you because my heart feels peaceful with you. Because even your presence in my thoughts makes my day lighter. ✨

This isn’t noise. This isn’t obsession. This isn’t fear.

It’s a quiet certainty.

And that certainty… is you ❤️
